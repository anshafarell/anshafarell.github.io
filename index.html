About Farell

Ananda Shaquille Farell is a third year Universitas Padjadjaran student majoring in Computer Science or Teknik Informatika. He is a creative and open-minded individual. He is open to new and exciting possibilities.

He is often a leader in any group project or task that he is in. The leadership spirit is in him.

Farell have a lot experience with C++, Java, Python, SQL, PHP, HTML, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. But, he does not limit himself to only that. He is eager to learn anything.

He is good with Canva, Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and other office softwares (such as Microsoft Word, Google Spreadsheet, and more!).

Feel free to reach him by any means of contact! He would be more than happy to receive it!
